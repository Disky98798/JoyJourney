About the Land Of Sandalwoods

Situated in the embrace of the Western Ghats and stretching its arms towards the Arabian Sea, the state of Karnataka beckons travellers with a symphony of landscapes, cultures, and history. This enchanting state, known for its heritage, wildlife, silk, spices, and sandalwood, unfolds like a treasure trove of experiences waiting to be discovered.Karnataka has many colleges and universities serving the needs of a large Indian population. The silicon valley of India is located in Karnataka. There are many scientific organisations located in Karnataka. Karnataka is the 6th largest state in India by area and 8th largest state in India by population.In addition to these, Karnataka proudly showcases over 25 other heritage sites such as Badami, Mysuru, Belur, Sringeri, and more. The state also boasts of a 58-feet tall statue of Gommateshwara, the world's largest monolithic statue, at Shravanbelagola.
