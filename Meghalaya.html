About the Abode Of Clouds

Meghalaya is mountainous, and it is the rainiest state of India. The word Meghalaya means, "abode of the clouds". Meghalaya is one of the Seven Sister States of northeast India. The state of Meghalaya is mountainous, with stretches of valley and highland plateaus, and it is geologically rich.Meghalaya has a rich and unique tribal culture. A major part of the tribal lives includes lively harvest festivals with mass dances in traditional attire, accompanied by music and dance from bamboo flutes, buffalo horns and drums. Christianity and Hinduism are the main religions of the state.The foggy hills, fresh valleys, and cascading waterfalls are perhaps the epitome of Meghalaya's beauty. That is why when you get to Cherrapunji you will realize why it is among the most beautiful places in Meghalaya.
